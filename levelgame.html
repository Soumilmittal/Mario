Super Mario Clone

How to Play

Use the Left and Right arrow keys (or on-screen buttons) to move.

Use the Up arrow key or Spacebar (or on-screen button) to jump.

Jump on enemies to defeat them and earn points.

Collect coins for extra points.

Reach the flag at the end of each level to proceed.

Avoid falling into pits or touching enemies from the side.
